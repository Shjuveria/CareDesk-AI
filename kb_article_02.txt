Title: Troubleshooting Cerner Printer Queue Failures
Category: Printing
Keywords: print job stuck, discharge papers, spooler, queue

1. Restart the print spooler service on the affected workstation.
2. Verify that the default printer is correctly mapped and selected in Cerner.
3. Reprint the document and monitor for errors in the local event viewer.

Notes:
Always check for hardware issues like low toner or paper jams before escalating to Cerner support.
