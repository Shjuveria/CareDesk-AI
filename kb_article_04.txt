Title: Orders Not Posting to Lab Interface
Category: Orders
Keywords: lab orders, interface, pending status, not transmitted

1. Verify the patient encounter is active and the order has a valid specimen source.
2. Confirm the interface status in Cerner (Interface Monitor Tool).
3. Resubmit the failed order or escalate to interface team if issue persists.

Notes:
Orders placed after midnight may face delays due to nightly batch processing.
