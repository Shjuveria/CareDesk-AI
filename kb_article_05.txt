Title: Reactivating Frozen Patient Charts
Category: Chart Access
Keywords: frozen chart, system hang, timeout, unresponsive

1. Instruct user to log out and log back in to clear cached session data.
2. Clear local Java/ActiveX cache (depends on Cerner client version).
3. Open the chart on a different workstation to isolate the issue.

Notes:
Performance issues may be tied to high-resolution imaging or excessive chart load.
